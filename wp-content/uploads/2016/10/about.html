Our Mission

To help homeless children by helping their families: to stay intact, to regain stable housing and to return to steady work, in the warm fold of a supportive, volunteer-driven community.

Our Vision

To empower local communities to end homelessness in the San Gabriel Valley.

Our Values

Hospitality, Empowerment, Compassion, Mobilizing Community, and Hope.

Our Story

Established in 2011, Family Promise of San Gabriel Valley is a nonprofit organization that touches lives by addressing family homelessness right here in the San Gabriel Valley community. with temporary housing and other assistance to homeless families with children.

In 2016, we're celebrating our fifth anniversary — looking forward to the next five years and beyond.

Family Promise National has come to represent more than 50,000 people in need every year and engage more than 180,000 volunteers.

It represents a national movement that believes we can address family homelessness. Family Promise SGV is only one of 202 local Family Promise affiliates in 41 states across the country.

Board of Directors

Karen Roberson
Chair

As a Licensed Clinical Social Worker, Karen has been able to work in a diverse range of settings, including healthcare social work, community social work, adoptions, and a rape hotline, which she directed.
